## Research Profile

I develop integrated ecological models to address biodiversity loss, human–wildlife conflict, and ecosystem management across farmland, riparian, grassland, boreal, and estuarine systems.

**Modelling & analytical methods**

`Integrated Population Modelling` · `Capture–Recapture Analyses` · `Process-Based Growth Dynamics` · `Functional Response & Food-Web Modelling` · `Machine-Learning SDMs (hierarchical/stacked)` · `Regime & Stability Analyses` · `Spatiotemporal Analyses` · `Bayesian & ODE Systems` · `Growth Metric / Outlier / Bias Correction`

**Published systems modelled**

| System | Output |
|--------|--------|
| Soil – Vegetation – Insect – Bird | 2 peer-reviewed papers |
| Tree – Deer – Wolf (Scandinavia) | Interactive Taiga-Climate app + 1 paper in communication |
| Phytoplankton – Zooplankton – Carp | 2 peer-reviewed papers |
| Canine Distemper Epidemic Control | 1 peer-reviewed paper |
| Probiotic – Helminth – Mammalian Body | 2 peer-reviewed papers |
| Tumour Cell – Antibiotic Systems | 2 peer-reviewed papers |

**Public databases used:** InsectBase · GBIF · eBird · iNaturalist · TRY · CMIP6 · CRU · ERA5 · EUR-11 · LUCAS · India-WRIS · Bhuvan  
**Software & languages:** Python · R · MATLAB · Stella · QGIS · ArcGIS · LaTeX  
**Wet-lab:** Plankton culture · Soil physico-chemical analyses · Soil microbe isolation · Fish-gut & bird-pellet content analyses · Western blot · PCR
